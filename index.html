Tom Bombadil Fan Page

Introduction

Tom Bombadil is a character in J.R.R. Tolkien's mythical universe known as "Middle-Earth". His first appearance was in a 1934 poem published in The Oxford Magazine called "The Adventures of Tom Bombadil". He also appears in the much more well-known "The Fellowship of the Ring" published in 1954, where he saves the hobbits on their way to Mordor and teaches them some valuable lessons and speaks in nonsensical ways. Sadly Tom Bombadil's story never made it to the big screen when Peter Jackson adapted the legendary trilogy and thus most fans have never heard of Tom. He is a quite enigmatic figure and said to be the oldest being in all of Middle-Earth.

Facts about Tom Bombadil
